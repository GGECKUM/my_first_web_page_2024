Software Engineering Degree - Year 4

The fourth year of the Software Engineering degree program focuses on advanced topics and practical experience. Students will engage in project management, mobile application development, artificial intelligence, and a capstone project to demonstrate their skills and knowledge.

Courses

  • Project Management
    • Learn about project management methodologies and tools.
    • Topics include project planning, scheduling, risk management, and team collaboration.
  • Mobile Application Development
    • Introduction to mobile app development for platforms such as Android and iOS.
    • Learn about mobile UI/UX design, development frameworks, and app deployment.
  • Artificial Intelligence
    • Study of AI concepts and techniques, including machine learning and neural networks.
    • Learn about AI applications in various domains such as natural language processing and computer vision.
  • Capstone Project
    • Work on a comprehensive project that integrates knowledge and skills acquired throughout the degree program.
    • Collaborate with peers to design, develop, and present a software solution to a real-world problem.
  • Elective Courses
    • Choose from a range of elective courses to explore specialized topics of interest.
    • Examples include cybersecurity, cloud computing, and advanced database systems.
